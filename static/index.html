CalTrack

Open source calorie tracker for AI clients. Take a photo of your food and your AI estimates the calories and logs them automatically.

Works with Claude, ChatGPT, and any AI Vision client. No dashboard, no app — just talk to your AI.

Get started

  1. Add the MCP server to your Claude config: https://caltrack.xplaya.com/mcp
  2. Ask Claude to register you
  3. Start logging meals by photo or description
